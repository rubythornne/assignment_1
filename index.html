Responsive Design

Responsive website design solves the problem of enabling websites to fit on devices with smaller screens. This also enables businesses to create and maintain one website that fits on a vast array of devices, rather than building a web site or app for multiple devices. The amount of maintenance to upkeep multiple websites or apps for a small business can be especially time consuming and take away time spent on furthering the business in other ways. This is especially important, as more and more smaller businesses want an online presence, but also want to reach customers that use other devices to purchase products online. Not only must it be easy for a business to maintain, but also look good at as it scales to fit on specific devices. Responsive design provides this capability for such businesses and will only get better and easier to use as online business expands into the future.
